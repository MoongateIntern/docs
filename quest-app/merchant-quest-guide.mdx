---
title: "Quest Merchant App"
description: "A step-by-step guide for issuing and redeeming points using our quest merchant app"
---

<Note>In-App Browser is not supported</Note>
<Info>
  This guide is for merchant/staff organizing an interactive quest in their event
</Info>

<Accordion title="Logging into the dashboard">
<Steps>
<Step title="Access your quest page on the portal">
- Log in to the moongate merchant portal (https://portal.moongate.id)
- Navigate to quests from the sidebar
<img src="/images/quest-app/quest navigate.png" />
- Click on your quest from the quest list
</Step>
<Step title="Get the access code and redirect to quest app">
- Copy the Access Code and Click the **Verify App** button
<img src="/images/quest-app/quest access.png" />
</Step>
<Step title="Log in to the quest dashboard">
- Paste the access code into the code field and press **SIGN IN**
<img width="300" src="/images/quest-app/quest login.png" />
</Step>
</Steps>
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Understanding the Dashboard">
<Info>
You should be logged in to access this section
</Info>
- Once you login, you will see the homepage of the quest app. Press the **CONTINUE** button to jump to the dashboard.
<img width="300" src="/images/quest-app/quest home.png" />
- The dashboard contains the two action buttons that let you issue or redeem points:
  - **ADD POINTS**
  - **REDEEM PRIZE**

  <img width="300" src="/images/quest-app/quest dashboard.png" />

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Issuing Points">
<Info>
You should be logged in to access this section
</Info>
- To issue points click the **ADD POINTS** button on the dashboard
<img width="300" src="/images/quest-app/quest dashboard add.png" />
- You will see a list of categories and quests that you can award/issue points to user on this page.
<img width="300" src="/images/quest-app/quest list add.png" />

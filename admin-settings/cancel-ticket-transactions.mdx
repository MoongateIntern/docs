---
title: "Cancel Ticket Transaction"
description: "Organizers can now offer a cancellation feature for tickets. To enable this feature, please request assistance from our team."
---

<Warning>
  For transactions that are cancelled, the refunds need to be done manually by
  the organiser for now!
</Warning>
